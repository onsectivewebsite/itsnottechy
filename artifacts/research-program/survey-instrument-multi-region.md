## Expected publication structure

Once fielded, findings to extract for the published report:

1. **The centralization spectrum** — what % of respondents run each model, and how that correlates with CAC and growth rate.
2. **Channel ranking divergence across regions** — which channels are most vs least durable across borders.
3. **Creative localization practices** — the 3 patterns that separate brands growing in multiple regions from those stuck in one.
4. **The compliance tax** — quantitative estimate of the revenue / program cost of regulatory compliance overhead.
5. **The attribution gap** — the specific measurement problems holding multi-region programs back.
