# Case Study Publication Consent

**Between:** [Client Company Name] ("Client")
**And:** It's Not Techy / Onsective Inc. ("Agency")

## Scope of consent

Client grants Agency permission to publish a case study describing Client's engagement with Agency, subject to the terms below.

### What may be published

Upon Client's written approval of the final draft, Agency may publicly publish:

- Client's company name and logo
- Industry and business description (as reviewed and approved)
- Engagement start and end dates, scope of services, and general methodology
- Specific performance metrics and outcomes as agreed in writing (e.g., "organic traffic grew X%")
- A pull quote attributed to a named Client representative, exact wording approved by Client
- Links to Client's publicly-available website or marketing properties

### What will not be published without separate written approval

- Revenue figures, dollar amounts of campaign spend, profit margins, or any confidential financial information
- Internal process, proprietary technology, employee names other than the approved spokesperson
- Strategic plans, competitive positioning against named competitors
- Anything not approved in the final reviewed draft

## Review and approval

- Agency will deliver a full draft of the case study to Client at least **14 business days** before intended publication.
- Client has **10 business days** to request revisions; changes requested are made prior to publication.
- Publication does not proceed until Client provides final written approval (email sufficient).

## Withdrawal

Client may, at any time after publication, request removal of the case study. Agency will remove or anonymize the published case study within **10 business days** of a withdrawal request delivered to info@itsnottechy.com. No further use of Client's name, logo, or quotes will occur after a withdrawal request.

## Ownership and reuse

Client retains ownership of its name, logo, trademarks, and any materials or data provided. Agency may reuse the published case study in:

- Agency's own marketing (website, sales decks, social, email)
- Industry roundups and third-party review platforms (Clutch, G2, GoodFirms, etc.)
- Pitch responses to prospective clients

Agency will not license or sell the case study to third parties without Client's additional written consent.

## No additional compensation

No monetary consideration is exchanged for this consent. Agency does not pay for testimonials.
