# Case study interview guide

**Duration:** 30 minutes
**Format:** Recorded on Riverside or Zoom (recording shared with client after)
**Preparation:** Send questions to client 3 business days in advance

## Opening (2 minutes)

- Confirm recording consent
- Confirm what's approved for publication vs. off-the-record
- "The goal here is to get specific enough that another operator reading this would know what we actually did and what moved."

## 1. Baseline — what was the situation before we started? (5 minutes)

- What was the state of [relevant metric / channel / program] when we started?
- What had you tried before that didn't work?
- What was the budget, team, or capacity constraint?
- What would have happened if we hadn't worked together?

## 2. Decision — why us? (3 minutes)

- What were the other options you considered?
- What convinced you to work with us specifically?
- What concern did you have that almost stopped the engagement?

## 3. The work — what did we actually do? (10 minutes)

- Walk me through the first 30 days. What did we prioritize?
- What was the one decision or change that had the biggest impact?
- What didn't work that we tried? What did we kill?
- What part of the engagement surprised you — good or bad?

## 4. Outcome — what changed? (5 minutes)

- What specific metrics moved?
- Over what timeframe?
- What's the downstream business impact — revenue, hires, customer perception?
- What's the current state, 6–12 months after engagement wrapped?

## 5. Reference questions (3 minutes)

- What would you tell another [industry] founder considering us?
- Is there a specific part of the engagement you'd cite as the reason it worked?
- Would you work with us again, and if so, on what?

## 6. Quote selection (2 minutes)

- "Out of what you just said, is there a line or two you'd be comfortable with us quoting publicly under your name and title?"
- Read it back. Get verbal confirmation.

## Post-interview

- Transcribe within 48 hours (Riverside does this automatically).
- Draft the case study using the template within 7 days.
- Send draft to client with the pull-quote highlighted and a note: "Review at your own pace — nothing ships until you sign off."
