## Description

It's Not Techy is a global digital marketing agency serving growth-stage B2B SaaS, DTC ecommerce, and professional services brands. Eight offices across Toronto (HQ), New York, London, Dubai, Mumbai, Singapore, Sydney, and Berlin. We're the official marketing partner of Onsective Inc., a global IT consultancy delivering enterprise engineering, cloud, AI, and digital transformation.
