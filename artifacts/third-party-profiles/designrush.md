## Value proposition (250 words)

It's Not Techy is a global digital marketing agency that delivers senior-led strategy and hands-on execution across the full marketing stack — web, SEO, content, paid, social, video, brand, marketing platforms, and AI tooling — for growth-stage B2B SaaS, DTC ecommerce, and professional services brands.

What makes us different: every account is led by a practitioner with at least a decade of specialized experience. There's no "pitched by senior, delivered by junior" bait-and-switch. Engagements are measured against pipeline, revenue, CAC, and payback — not impressions, reach, or vanity metrics.

Our eight offices across Toronto, New York, London, Dubai, Mumbai, Singapore, Sydney, and Berlin mean every engagement is staffed with local practitioners who understand the region's compliance rules, language, and culture — critical for multi-region programs where GDPR, PIPEDA, DPDP, and regional consumer protection rules shape what's possible.

Engagements run as fixed-fee Sprints (2–4 weeks, $5K–$25K, single workstream), Programs (3–12 months, monthly retainer $15K–$80K, multi-channel), or Embedded (ongoing fractional CMO + specialist pod, $30K+ monthly). Media cost is always broken out from our fee — we never take a percentage of your ad spend. Clients retain ownership of all files, accounts, and data; there's no vendor lock-in.

We operate as the official marketing partner of Onsective Inc., a global IT consultancy that handles enterprise engineering, cloud, AI, and digital transformation for our shared clients.
