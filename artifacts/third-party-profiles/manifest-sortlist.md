# The Manifest + Sortlist

These are second-tier but still worth the 30 minutes of submission time each.

## The Manifest

**URL:** https://themanifest.com/add-your-agency
**Operator:** Same parent as Clutch (owned by The Manifest / Clutch.co)
**Strategic value:** Shares Clutch's review infrastructure, so reviews collected on one often surface on the other. Good for SEO backlink diversity.

### Content

Use the **Clutch profile content** at `artifacts/third-party-profiles/clutch.md` as-is. The Manifest accepts the same fields and pulls largely the same schema.

## Sortlist

**URL:** https://www.sortlist.com/agencies/sign-up
**Operator:** Belgium-based; strong in European markets
**Strategic value:** Primary directory used by European brands searching for agency partners. Pairs especially well with the London, Berlin, and Dubai offices.
