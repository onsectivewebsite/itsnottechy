# Third-party profile submissions

Third-party citations consistently outweigh on-site content for AI visibility. When ChatGPT or Perplexity is asked "best digital marketing agency for B2B SaaS," it pulls from Clutch, G2, GoodFirms, DesignRush, TrustRadius, Wikipedia, and Reddit far more often than from agency websites themselves.

This folder contains ready-to-submit content for each major platform. Each file is platform-appropriate — profile-length for directories, Wikipedia-style neutral prose for Wikipedia, etc.

## Submission checklist

1. **Clutch** — https://clutch.co/sign-up-agency (paid tier recommended for verified reviews)
2. **G2** — https://www.g2.com/products/new (agency listings are free; best paired with a claimable Service Provider profile)
3. **GoodFirms** — https://www.goodfirms.co/join (free listing + optional verified reviews)
4. **DesignRush** — https://www.designrush.com/apply (curated — you apply; they review)
5. **Capterra / Software Advice** — https://www.capterra.com/vendors/ (relevant for SaaS marketing + marketing automation tooling positioning)
6. **TrustRadius** — https://www.trustradius.com/vendors
7. **The Manifest** — https://themanifest.com/add-your-agency (Clutch's sister site)
8. **Sortlist** — https://www.sortlist.com/agencies/sign-up (European strength, good for EMEA positioning)
9. **Wikipedia** — not a direct submission; article drafted as starting point for a volunteer editor

## What to pair with each submission

Most review directories require 3–5 client reviews to verify a profile. Feed those from the case-studies pipeline in `artifacts/case-study-outreach/`. Each case study client who consents publicly should also be asked for a review on 1–2 relevant directories.

## Prioritization

If you can only submit to three platforms this quarter:

1. **Clutch** — most frequently cited by AI systems in the agency category
2. **G2** — strongest for B2B SaaS discovery; high domain authority
3. **GoodFirms** — quickest approval, adds backlink volume

The rest compound over 6–12 months.
