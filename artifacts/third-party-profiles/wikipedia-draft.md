## Notes for the commissioning editor

When hiring a Wikipedia editor:

- Engage a **declared paid contributor** via the [Wikipedia Paid Editing Policy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Paid-contribution_disclosure). Paid editors must disclose the relationship. Undisclosed paid editing results in article deletion and reputational damage.
- Budget approximately **$1,500–$4,000** for a qualified editor. Cheaper offers (the $300 "I'll create your Wikipedia page" offers) almost always result in speedy-deletion outcomes and can trigger COI investigation.
- The editor should submit via [Articles for Creation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Articles_for_creation), not directly into mainspace.
- Review turnaround is typically 2–8 weeks for AfC submissions.
