## Full description

It's Not Techy is a global digital marketing agency built around a simple idea: senior practitioners lead every engagement, and every campaign is measured against pipeline and revenue — not impressions.

We serve growth-stage brands, typically $1M–$100M ARR, across B2B SaaS, DTC ecommerce, fintech, and professional services. Our service range covers web design and development (Next.js, Shopify, Webflow, headless commerce), SEO (technical, on-page, content, local, AI search optimization), paid marketing (Meta, Google, LinkedIn, TikTok, programmatic), content and copywriting, social media and community, video production, brand design, marketing platforms (HubSpot, Salesforce, Klaviyo, Segment), and AI marketing (custom GPTs, agent workflows, LLM SEO).

Our eight offices cover the major global time zones: Toronto (Global HQ), New York (Americas Hub), London (EMEA Hub), Dubai (Middle East Hub), Mumbai (APAC Delivery Center), Singapore (APAC Hub), Sydney (Oceania Hub), and Berlin (EU Engineering). Each office staffs local practitioners who understand regional compliance (GDPR, PIPEDA, CASL, DPDP), language, and cultural context.

## Key services detail (paragraphs per service)

### SEO Services

Full-stack SEO including technical audits, on-page optimization, content strategy, local SEO, and AI search optimization (GEO / LLM-SEO). Delivered as a 12-month minimum engagement. Typical outcomes: 3–10x organic traffic within 12 months on a well-scoped program, top-3 rankings for money keywords, and reduced dependency on paid acquisition.

### PPC and Paid Media

Full-funnel paid media across Google, Meta, LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube, and programmatic. Engineered for CAC reduction and profitable scaling — creative production in-house, tracking and attribution audited at engagement start. We report on blended MER and new-customer contribution margin, not just in-platform ROAS.

### Web Design and Development

Custom websites on Next.js, Webflow, and Shopify (or headless). Built for sub-1s LCP, strong Core Web Vitals, and schema-first SEO foundations. Marketing sites typically ship in 4–8 weeks; complex ecommerce or web apps in 8–16 weeks.

### AI Marketing

Custom GPTs, AI SDR systems, LLM-SEO, and workflow automation with Claude, GPT, and open-source models. We implement AI across content, SDR outreach, CX, SEO, and analytics — with human-in-the-loop governance for quality-sensitive work.
