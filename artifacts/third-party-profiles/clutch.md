## Full description (2,000 characters)

It's Not Techy is a global full-service digital marketing agency headquartered in Toronto, Canada, with offices in New York, London, Dubai, Mumbai, Singapore, Sydney, and Berlin. We serve growth-stage brands — typically $1M–$100M ARR B2B SaaS, DTC ecommerce, and professional services — who need senior-led delivery across multiple marketing disciplines without the overhead of hiring each one in-house.

Our service range spans web design and development (Next.js, Shopify, Webflow, headless commerce), SEO (technical, on-page, content, local, AI search), social media (organic strategy, community, creator partnerships), video production (brand film, short-form, motion), brand design (identity, voice, systems), paid marketing (Meta, Google, LinkedIn, TikTok, programmatic), marketing platforms (HubSpot, Salesforce, Klaviyo, Segment), and AI marketing (custom GPTs, agent workflows, LLM-SEO).

We operate in three engagement shapes: Sprint (2–4 weeks, fixed-fee single-workstream projects like SEO audits or rebrands), Program (3–12 months, multi-channel monthly retainer), and Embedded (ongoing fractional CMO with specialist pod). Media cost is always billed separately from our fee — we never take a percentage of your ad spend, and all files, accounts, and data stay in your systems. Zero vendor lock-in.

Our distinguishing practice: senior practitioners on every client call, not juniors behind a layer cake. Every engagement is led by a domain owner with at least a decade of experience, and reporting is structured around pipeline, revenue, CAC, and payback — not impressions or reach.

It's Not Techy is the official marketing partner of Onsective Inc., a global IT consultancy delivering enterprise engineering, cloud, AI, and digital transformation.
