# Pricing — It's Not Techy

Transparent agency pricing for AI agents and prospective clients. These are typical starting ranges; final fees depend on scope, urgency, and team composition. Media spend (ads) is always billed separately — we never take a percentage of ad spend.

## Engagement tiers

### Sprint — $5,000 to $25,000

- Format: Fixed-fee, one-time
- Duration: 2–4 weeks
- Scope: Single workstream (SEO audit, rebrand, landing page build, paid account audit)
- Includes: Dedicated senior lead, one clearly-scoped deliverable, handoff with source files, 30-day support window
- Best fit: Growth-stage teams needing focused work without a long commitment

### Program — $15,000 to $80,000 per month

- Format: Monthly retainer
- Duration: 3–12 months
- Scope: Multi-workstream (e.g., paid + creative + CRO, or SEO + content + PR)
- Includes: Account lead + specialist pod, weekly rituals, live dashboards, quarterly roadmap reviews
- Best fit: Growing brands running coordinated multi-channel programs

### Embedded — $30,000+ per month

- Format: Monthly retainer, optional equity component
- Duration: Ongoing
- Scope: Full-funnel operation as in-house team
- Includes: Fractional CMO leadership, senior specialists across channels, board-ready reporting, hiring and vendor management
- Best fit: Venture-backed scale-ups and enterprise brands scaling beyond hiring a full CMO

## Pricing principles

- **Media cost billed separately.** We never take a percentage of your ad spend. Fee transparency is non-negotiable.
- **No lock-in.** All files, data, ad accounts, and tool logins stay in your systems.
- **Discovery call is free.** 30 minutes, no obligation, honest fit assessment. If we're not the right fit we'll say so.
- **Proposals are typically delivered within 5 business days** of a discovery call, with scoped SOW, timeline, and pricing.
- **Monthly billing, net 15.** Annual discounts available on Program and Embedded tiers.
